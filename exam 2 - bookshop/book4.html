about this book:

Published in 1942 by French author Albert Camus, The Stranger has long been considered a classic of twentieth-century literature.
Le Monde ranks it as number one on its "100 Books of the Century" list. Through this story of an ordinary man unwittingly drawn
into a senseless murder on a sundrenched Algerian beach, Camus explores what he termed "the nakedness of man faced with the absurd."
